We are excited to launch the official website of the **Mis|Disinformation Initiative** at the Amsterdam School of Communication Research, University of Amsterdam.

## About This Website

This platform serves as a hub for:

- **Research Collaboration**: Connecting researchers studying mis- and disinformation
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Disseminating findings and insights from our work
- **Education**: Promoting our educational programs and resources
- **Engagement**: Facilitating dialogue with stakeholders and the broader community

## What You'll Find Here

- **[Projects](/projects/)**: Our ongoing research projects
- **[Publications](/publications/)**: Academic papers and research outputs
- **[Education](/education/)**: Information about our courses and training programs
- **News & Events**: Updates on conferences, workshops, and seminars (you're here!)

## Get Involved

We welcome collaboration and engagement from researchers, educators, practitioners, students, and anyone interested in addressing information disorders. Explore our site and reach out to connect!
